Miguel Angel Pardo, a.k.a Zas Mooncat,

With a distinctive style in both his productions and live sets,
the Valencian DJ / Producer Mooncat stands out as one of the leading
figures in the Spanish raggajungle scene. As a producer he characterizes
his sound as "neuro dubwize" - combining the rhythms and
structure of neurofunk with the atmospheres and reggae samples of dubwize.

During his long and fruitful career he has worked with and remixed
artists such as General Levy & Junior Kelly, and can look back upon an
impressive array of original productions, including vinyl releases on
internationally acclaimed labels such as Melting Pot (SP) Step Express
(FR) & Dubwise Station (FR/UK) to name a few. His most recent release
on Melting Pot reached #1 in the charts on Juno Records where it stayed
for over two weeks - an undeniable sign of his talent for high quality
production.

South Yard es un sello musical dedicado a la música electrónica
fundado en 2015 por parte de Mooncat, Karlixx, Kursiva y Laura Pichiflay
con la intención de promover la escena del drum&bass a nivel internacional
apostando sobretodo por el talento nacional y por las nuevas promesas.
Después de mas de 50 lanzamientos, South Yard se posiciona como uno de los
sellos de drum&bass mas influyentes de la escena actual, contando con lanzamientos
de artistas como Conrad Subs, Speaker Louis, True Tactix... y por supuesto
de los propios fundadores Kursiva, Karlixx y Mooncat entre otros.
